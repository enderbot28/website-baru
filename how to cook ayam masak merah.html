Baked Salmon with Roasted Vegetables Ingredients: 4 salmon fillets 1 lb of mixed vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers) 2 tbsp of olive oil 1 tbsp of garlic powder 1 tbsp of dried oregano Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces and toss them with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper. Arrange the vegetables on a baking sheet and roast them in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until tender and slightly caramelized. While the vegetables are roasting, season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper. Heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the salmon fillets, skin-side down, and cook for 3-4 minutes until the skin is crispy. Flip the salmon fillets over and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the salmon is cooked through. Serve the salmon fillets with the roasted vegetables on the side. Enjoy your delicious and healthy dinner!
